✗ Apologize for demo limitations
✗ Say "this is just a prototype"
✗ Spend too long on any one scenario
✗ Skip the metrics review
✗ Forget to mention data sovereignty

DO:
✓ Focus on business value
✓ Use specific numbers
✓ Pause for questions between scenarios
✓ Point to screen actively
✓ Emphasize "automatic" and "real-time"
✓ Connect to their pain points
