Summary
The PoC scope is to build a middle-office orchestration and validation layer that preprocesses payments, dynamically routes transactions across rails (SWIFT, PayShap, RTGS), and converts messages to MX. It addresses legacy architecture load, modernization, customer experience abstraction, and AI-driven optimization. Pre-validation rules are shifted left to reduce back-office strain, while AI ensures intelligent routing, fraud detection, and future-proofing.

Scope of PoC
1. Rails Covered
Cross-border: SWIFT (MT → MX conversion), SADC rails, partner-driven rails (e.g., Remitly, CIPS).

Domestic: PayShap (instant/scheduled, non-bulk) and RTGS (high-value, bulk).

Future-proofing: EFT deprecation (2026–2027) → AI model trained now for seamless transition.

2. Use Cases Proposed
Payment Preprocessing (Order Management)

Left-shift validations currently done in back-office.

Reduce load on legacy systems and insulate them from channel modernization.

Intelligent Payment Routing

Dynamic rail selection based on customer choice (“fastest vs cheapest”).

AI-driven routing logic considering liquidity, compliance, risk, and cost optimization.

Dynamic Message Conversion

MT to MX (ISO20022) and custom format conversion.

Supports international and domestic rails moving to MX.
